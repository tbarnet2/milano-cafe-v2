Welcome to Milano Cafe

Milano Cafe combines authentic Italian recipes, quality ingredients, and warm hospitality to create a memorable dining experience in Southwest Austin.

Our restaurant is built around the belief that great food brings people together. Every meal is prepared with care and served in a relaxed, welcoming atmosphere.

Whether you're joining us for lunch, dinner, a special celebration, or an evening of live music, we strive to make every visit enjoyable and memorable.

What Makes Milano Cafe Special

Authentic Italian Cuisine

Traditional recipes, handcrafted dishes, fresh pasta, and artisan pizza.

Live Music

Regular live performances create a unique atmosphere that enhances the dining experience.

Local Art

Milano Cafe showcases local artwork and supports Austin's creative community.

Our Mission

To serve exceptional Italian food while creating a place where friends, families, and neighbors can gather and connect.
